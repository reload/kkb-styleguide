7.0 Images & Text

The image wrapper has a .shrink class. When breakpoint is met, the image begins to shrink.

Biblioteket
Rentemestervej

Rentemestervej 76
2400 København NV

+45 82321600
bibbro@kff.kk.dk

7.2 Image underline & image round corners

The image text wrapper has a .imgtext-underline class. That gives the content an underline. Give the image .img-round for rounded corners.

Litteratur og lydbøger

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Optio, porro!

<div class="imgtext underline">
    <div class="img-wrap">
            <a href="#"><img src="_/img/content/60x60.png" class="img-responsive img-round"></a>
        </div>
        <div class="text">
            <h4 class="big">Litteratur og lydbøger</h4>
            <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Optio, porro!</p>
        </div>
</div>
